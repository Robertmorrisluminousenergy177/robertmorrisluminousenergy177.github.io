Research

Three questions I keep coming back to, plus selected publications.

01

Neural clocks

How do populations of cortical neurons represent elapsed time without a dedicated "clock" region? We model timing as an emergent property of recurrent dynamics.

recurrent networkselectrophysiology
02

Timing under uncertainty

Bayesian models of how humans estimate duration when sensory evidence is noisy — and why our estimates are systematically biased toward the mean.

Bayesian inferencepsychophysics
03

Timing in disease

Disorders like Parkinson's disease disrupt interval timing early. We use this as a behavioral biomarker and a window into basal ganglia function.
