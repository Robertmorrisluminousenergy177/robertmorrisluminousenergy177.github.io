Education

Degrees, training, and the institutions along the way.

Postdoctoral Fellow, Computational Neuroscience

Alderidge Institute for Brain Science

2018

Ph.D. in Neuroscience

Marlowe University

Dissertation: "Bayesian accounts of duration perception across sensory modalities." Advisor: Dr. R. Kessler. Combined EEG and psychophysics to characterize systematic biases in human timing judgments.

2013

M.Sc. in Cognitive Science

Marlowe University

Thesis on sequence learning in working memory tasks. Coursework in computational modeling, statistics, and systems neuroscience.

2011

B.Sc. in Psychology, Minor in Mathematics

Northfield College

Graduated with honors. Undergraduate thesis on rhythm perception in musicians vs. non-musicians, advised by Dr. P. Adeyemi.
